Philippine Income Tax Brackets

  • 0% for income up to PHP 250,000
  • 20% for income between PHP 250,001 and PHP 400,000
  • 25% for income between PHP 400,001 and PHP 800,000
  • 30% for income between PHP 800,001 and PHP 2,000,000
  • 32% for income between PHP 2,000,001 and PHP 8,000,000
  • 35% for income over PHP 8,000,000
